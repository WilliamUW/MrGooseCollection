UWFlow.com

Find information about the courses you want to enroll here! You can search courses by their "Useful", "Easy" and "Liked" ratings, as well as the professors that teach them.
UWPath.live

Plan your degree with UWPath! UWPath is a degree planning website built for UWaterloo students. You can plan your courses in our degree planner, where we verify prerequisites, corequisites, antirequisites and degree fulfillment on your behalf.
